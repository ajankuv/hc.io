Sample hardware list below

This is the list of hardware we used to develop the software, the only crutial thing of the entire setup is the raspberry pi. The rest can be changed and modified to your liking based on your needs. This is a simple 4 outlet design by Festiva500 on thingiverse for 10amps. Which is more then enough to run an LED light for my project and a water pump.
  • CanaKit Raspberry Pi 3 with 2.5A Micro USB Power Supply (UL Listed)
  • SainSmart 4-Channel Relay Module or from here (Slow and cheaper from china)
  • Female to Female Breadboard Jumper wires or from here (Slow and cheaper from china)
  • 1 x DigiKey Q212-ND
  • 4 x DigiKey Q227-ND
  • 1 x DigiKey RA11131100-ND
  • 2 x 3mm x 25mm hex head cap screws
  • 4 x 3mm x 8mm hex head cap screws
  • The 3d printed case can be found here for everything. If you do not have a 3d printer you can source a case here.

All of this can be sourced from anywhere these are just generic options. You can upgrade whatever you like to substain 20amp or lower. The software will work the same for any of it. It just needs the raspberry pi and a gpio relay to work.
